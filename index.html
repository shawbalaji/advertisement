About Prachar


In the heart of Bihar, where tradition meets transformation, Prachar was born in 2020. Founded by Harsh Kumar it was more than a business—it was a vision to redefine our urban landscapes and make local businesses more visible.

Today, we are Bihar’s premier Digital Out-of-Home (DOOH) network. We combine prime digital and static billboard inventory with dedicated on-ground teams in Patna, Gaya, and Muzaffarpur. We aren't just an agency; we are your brand guardians, ensuring your message makes a real-world impact.
